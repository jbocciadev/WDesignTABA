  • Additional Cover Options:
  • Accidental damage cover
  • Add accidental damage as an optional extra to your home insurance policy. This covers accidental damage to your building and/or contents.
  • Cover for Portable items
  • Cover for portable items such as laptops, mobile phones, tablets and jewellery outside of the home.
  • Legal Expenses Cover
  • Cover to help with legal costs arising from certain disputes, such as property or neighbour disputes.
  • Alternative Accommodation Cover
  • Cover to pay for alternative accommodation if your home becomes uninhabitable following an insured event.
  • Family Legal Protection
  • Cover to help with legal costs arising from certain personal legal disputes, such as employment or personal injury disputes.
  • Home Emergency Cover
  • Cover to arrange and pay for emergency repairs to your home following an insured event, such as a burst pipe or a broken window.
  • Golf Equipment Cover
  • Cover for your golf equipment, including when it is outside of the home.
  • Bike Cover
  • Cover for your bicycles, including when they are outside of the home.
  • Personal Accident Cover
  • Cover to pay a lump sum if you suffer a serious injury following an accident.
  • Emergency Home Assistance Cover
  • Cover to arrange and pay for emergency repairs to your home following an insured event, such as a burst pipe or a broken window.
  • Domestic Help Cover
  • Cover to pay for domestic help if you are unable to carry out your normal domestic duties following an accident or illness.
  • Garden Cover
  • Cover for damage to your garden, including plants, trees and shrubs.
  • Personal Belongings Cover
  • Cover for personal belongings such as clothing, jewellery and sports equipment outside of the home.

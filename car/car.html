Cool Insurance - Terms and Conditions

1. Coverage

Coverage for Life, Home, and Motor Insurance is provided as outlined in each policy schedule. Limits, benefits, and exclusions vary by product.

2. Premiums

Premiums must be paid by the due date. Failure to pay may result in suspension or cancellation of the policy.

3. Claims

Claims must be submitted promptly with any supporting documents requested by Cool Insurance. Claim approval is subject to policy terms.

4. Exclusions

General and product-specific exclusions apply. Losses outside the scope of cover are not insured.

5. Changes to Policy

Cool Insurance may update policy terms when required. Continued use of the policy indicates acceptance of any changes.

6. Cancellation

Either party may cancel the policy according to the cancellation rules in the policy schedule.

7. Liability

Cool Insurance’s liability is limited to the amounts and conditions stated in the applicable policy.
